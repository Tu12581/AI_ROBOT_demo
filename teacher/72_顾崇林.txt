(实审中) Biography    Dr. Chonglin Gu is an assistant professor at the school of computer science and technology in Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen). Dr. Gu got B.S. degree in computer science and technology from Harbin Engineering University, and then got M.S. and Ph.D. degree in computer science and technology from Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen). Dr. Gu got Excellent Doctoral Dissertation Award of Shenzhen Campus during the 100th anniversary of Harbin Institute of Technology (Top 8 in Computer Science). 2018/05 - 2018/08, Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data, Visiting Student. 2018/09 - 2020/08, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen，Postdoctor. 2020/09 - Now, Harbin Institue of Technology (Shenzhen), Assistant Professor. Research Direction: Cloud Computing, Green Scheduling for Data Center，Security in Cloud Storage. Dr. Gu has already published 20+ academic papers, including top journal paper in cloud computing area (IEEE Transactions on Cloud Computing，Future Generation Computing Systems) and CCF recommended conference papers such as ICC, ISCC, ICA3PP, IPCCC, ISPA and awared Best Student  in ISPA2016. In addition, two patents are granted and one book chapter has been published. As project investigator (PI), Dr. Gu got three fundings, including the First Class Grant in 2019 Chinese Postdoctoral Science Foundation（Top 6）and the Special Grant in 2020 Chinese Postdoctoral Science Foundation (Top15).
