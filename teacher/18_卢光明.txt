From 2005 to 2007, he was a Postdoctoral Fellow at Tsinghua University. Now, He is a Professor at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, China. He has published over 200 technical papers at some international journals and conferences, including IJCV、TIP、TNNLS、TCYB、TMM、TCSVT、TSMC, NeurIPS, CVPR, AAAI, IJCAI, etc. His research interests include computer vision, pattern recognition, and machine learning. : 2000-2005 D.E. in computer science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology, China. 1998-2000 M.E.Sc. in control theory and control engineering, Harbin Institute of Technology, China. 1994-1998 B.E. in automation of industry, Harbin Institute of Technology, China.
