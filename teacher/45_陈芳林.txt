Biography    Fanglin Chen received the B.S. degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University, Xi'an, China, in 2006. In 2011, he received the Ph.D. degree from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China. He is currently an associate professor with the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen. His research areas include computer vision, and machine learning. He received the Academic Newcomer Award (Ministry of , China) in 2010, and the First Group of Robot Vision Challenge (ImageCLEF) in 2014. "
